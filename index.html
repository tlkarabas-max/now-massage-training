      Online Booking Adjustment

To ensure you have the most relaxing experience with us, would you be available for either [TIME OPTION 1] or [TIME OPTION 2] instead of your original [ORIGINAL TIME]? Both times will allow us to provide you with our full attention and service. Please let us know which works best for you! The NOW Team

      Group Booking Coordination

      Hi [Guest Name], We're excited to accommodate your group of [NUMBER]! To ensure everyone can enjoy their services together, we can offer: • Option 1: [TIME] - All [NUMBER] guests together • Option 2: [TIME 1] - [X] guests, then [TIME 2] - remaining guests • Option 3: Split across [TIME 1] and [TIME 2] with overlapping relaxation time Which option would work best for your group? Looking forward to providing you all with a wonderful experience! The NOW Team

      Gap Prevention Call

      Hello [Guest Name], This is [Your Name] from The NOW. I'm calling about your [SERVICE] appointment tomorrow at [TIME]. We've had a cancellation that would allow us to accommodate you at [BETTER TIME], which would give you: • Less wait time upon arrival • Your preferred therapist • A more peaceful environment Would you like to move to this preferable time slot? [If yes]: Perfect! I've updated your appointment. [If no]: No problem! We'll see you at your original time. Thank you!

      Walk-In Optimization

      Welcome to The NOW! We can get you in for your [SERVICE] at: • [IMMEDIATE TIME] - Available now with [THERAPIST 1] • [OPTIMAL TIME] - In [X] minutes with our lead therapist • [LATER TIME] - Your choice of any therapist The [OPTIMAL TIME] would ensure the most relaxing experience as it's during our quieter period. Which would you prefer?
      Pro Tips for Using Scripts:
      • Always offer multiple options (people prefer choosing)
      • Frame changes as benefits to the guest
      • Be specific about advantages (preferred therapist, quieter time, etc.)
      • Keep tone friendly and accommodating
      • Document successful scripts for team use
