Script Templates

Phone Call Script:
“Hi [Name]! This is [Experience Guide] calling from The NOW [Boutique]. How are you today?
I’m calling about your upcoming appointment on [Date]. We’re optimizing our schedule and wanted to see if you could move your appointment to [Option 1] or [Option 2] instead of your original time at [Original Time]. Would either of those work for you?”
Text Message Script:
“Hi [Name]! This is the NOW [Boutique] reaching out about your appointment on [Date] with [Therapist Name].
We’re hoping you can begin your service 30 minutes earlier at [New Time].
Please call us at [Phone Number] or reply to this text to confirm. Thank you!”
Email Template:
Subject: Appointment Adjustment Request
Body:
Dear [Name],
We are reviewing our schedule for [Date] and would like to offer you an earlier appointment at [New Time].
This helps us provide the best experience for all guests and maximize therapist availability.
Please reply or call us at [Phone Number] to confirm your new time.
In-Person Script (Post-Service Rebooking):
“Hi [Name], how was your experience today? Your Massage Therapist recommends coming back in [Weeks].
Based on our schedule, we suggest booking your next appointment on [Day] at [Time].
Would that work for you, or is there another time you prefer?”
Guest Declines Change Script:
“We understand if the new time doesn’t work for you.
If you’d like, we can keep your original appointment.
Thank you for your flexibility!”

Zenoti Scheduling Blocks Reference Guide

Color-Coded Scheduling Blocks: Reference the definitions, color codes, and inclusion/exclusion rules below. Use these blocks to build optimal therapist schedules and maximize boutique revenue.

Color Reason Definition Included in Scheduled Service Hours?
Purple BREAK Required PAID break for employees, mandated by labor laws and company policy. No
Grey LUNCH Designated 30-minute UNPAID meal break, scheduled midway through shift. No
Grey PERSONAL TIME Pre-approved UNPAID time off for personal matters (appointments, errands, family). No
Purple PTO (Paid Time Off) Approved paid time off (vacations, mental health, personal days). No
Purple ADMIN Reserved time for administrative duties assigned
